Welcome to Advanced VLSI!
Introduction

This Virtual Lab program is designed as a second level course in VLSI after completion of basic courses. A student should have knowledge Basic Electronic Circuits and Introduction to VLSI to get maximum profit from this lab program. The lab contrues the notion of developing integrated circuits and optimizing various parameters of its performance. It consist of 10 Labs each covering various aspects of VLSI design and applications. This program will help the students to understand the basic notion behind Full Custom and Semi Custom Design design. They will get an insight into the notion of realising logic circuits on Generic Architectures. The Lab includes Basic Datapath Circuits and strategies for their power performance optimization. This Lab includes a simulator where you can actually simulate the design for a particular input data and see various time and power characteristics and output response of the circuits. So all the best for the experiments.

List of experiments
  • Full Custom Design
  • Semi Custom Design
  • Power Reduction Using Gated Clocking
  • Ripple Carry Adder
  • Carry Look Ahead Adder
  • 5-3 Compressor
  • Array Multiplier
  • (8,4) Hamming Coder
  • Linear Feedback Shift Register (LFSR)
Target Audience

The experiments are targeted at students who have just finished courses like Digital Logic Design and Processors, Basic Electronic Circuits, Introductory VLSI.

Courses Aligned

The present lab is aligned with courses such as Introduction to Digital Logic Design and Processors, Basic Electronic Circuits, Introductory VLSI. The experiments touch on most topics covered in such courses in most curricula.
